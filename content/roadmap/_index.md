## Phase 1: Transaction Integrity (Current)

- BIAN-compliant service domains
- Saga-based payment orchestration
- Lien-based fund reservation
- Double-entry accounting foundation

## Phase 2: Production Hardening

- ISO 20022 compliance layer
- Multi-tenancy (Organization service)
- Comprehensive audit logging

## Phase 3: Universal Assets (Future)

- Generic Quantity Type System
- Dynamic Asset Registry
- Support for energy, commodities, carbon credits
