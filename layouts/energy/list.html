One Read, Two Valuations

Your customer pays a fixed rate. You pay wholesale. Same kWh, different values.

Double-entry bookkeeping. Every meter read creates two positions: revenue at the customer's tariff, cost at the GSP wholesale price. The spread between contract and wholesale is your margin - visible at the moment of consumption, not month-end.

Meter Read Position
100 kWh → Revenue: 100 kWh × 24p = £24.00 Cost: 100 kWh × 28p = £28.00 Margin: -£4.00

This customer is underwater. You know it now, not at month-end.
